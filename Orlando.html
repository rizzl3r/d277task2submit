Orlando

The theme park king!

Quick Facts

  • Population: 307,573 (July 1, 2022)
  • Year of Incorporation: 1887 (the final incorporation)
  • Region: Central
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $66,292 (compared to state average, $67,917)

Looking for an exciting destination to explore? Consider Orlando, Florida! Renowned for its world-class theme parks, vibrant entertainment scene, and diverse attractions, Orlando offers an array of experiences for visitors of all ages. Whether you're seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures at thrilling rides, unwinding amidst scenic landscapes, or immersing yourself in cultural experiences, Orlando has it all. Come discover the magic of Orlando and create unforgettable moments that will last a lifetime!
