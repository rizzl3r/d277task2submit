Welcome to our website about Florida, your guide to the Sunshine State. Florida has been a part of the United States since March of 1845, and is the only state to have coasts on both the Atlantic Ocean and the Gulf of Mexico. Immerse yourself in the culture of Tallahassee, the state capital, where both history and progress coexist. Explore the vibrant art scene, take in the rich Southern charm, and uncover the political and cultural significance that defines this city.

Head to one of Central Florida's main cities, Tampa, where the Gulf Coast's energy meets a diverse cultural landscape. Experience the fusion of Cuban, Spanish, and Italian influences, and indulge in the thriving culinary scene and some great breweries, also. Tampa's vibrant waterfront, cultural institutions, and lively events make it a must-visit destination for every traveler.

Did you know?

Florida is home to the fabled "Florida Man"!

In the heart of Central Florida, Orlando awaits to host you with it's theme parks. Dive into the world of Walt Disney World and Universal Studios, experiencing unparalleled entertainment and creating lasting memories. For a cheaper experience, enjoy Disney Springs where families can walk and shop without paying any entrance fees.
