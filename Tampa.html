Tampa

Where every day is a vacation!

Quick Facts

  • Population: 398,173 (July 1, 2022)
  • Year of Incorporation: 1887 (the final incorporation)
  • Region: Central
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $66,802 (compared to state average, $67,917)

Looking for an ideal destination to explore? Look no further than Tampa, Florida! This vibrant city offers a plethora of exciting activities for all interests. From relaxing on pristine beaches to enjoying thrilling theme parks and catching exciting sports events, Tampa has something for everyone. With its consistently pleasant weather, you can enjoy outdoor adventures year-round. Don't miss out on the opportunity to experience the charm and excitement of Tampa firsthand!
